Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical exterior renovation take?
The duration of an exterior renovation can vary depending on the scope of the project. A simple siding replacement might take 1-2 weeks, while a complete exterior overhaul could take 4-6 weeks or more. We always provide a detailed timeline before starting any project.
Is stucco suitable for all climate types?
Stucco is versatile and can be used in most climates. However, it performs best in dry, warm areas. In regions with high humidity or frequent freeze-thaw cycles, special considerations may be necessary.
How often does stucco need to be maintained?
With proper installation, stucco requires minimal maintenance. It's recommended to inspect annually and clean every 2-3 years. Repainting may be needed every 5-10 years, depending on environmental factors.
Can stucco be applied over existing siding?
In many cases, yes. Stucco can be applied over materials like wood, brick, or concrete. However, a professional assessment is necessary to ensure proper adhesion and prevent future issues.
What's the difference between traditional and synthetic stucco?
Traditional stucco is made from cement, lime, sand, and water, while synthetic stucco (EIFS) uses acrylic resins and other additives. Synthetic stucco is more flexible and offers better insulation, but traditional stucco is often more durable and cost-effective.
